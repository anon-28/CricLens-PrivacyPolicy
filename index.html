CricLens Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2025

CricLens helps you analyze cricket performance using video. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Account & onboarding data: Your name (optional) to personalize the experience.
  • Videos you import or record: Used to run analysis and generate results.
  • Analysis outputs: Pose frames, metrics, tips, and session history saved on-device.
  • Device permissions: Camera and microphone access for recording, storage access for saving exports.

How We Use Your Data

  • Run on-device analysis to generate performance insights.
  • Store sessions locally so you can review your history.
  • Export overlay videos to your device when you choose to download them.

Data Storage

CricLens stores analysis sessions, metrics, and preferences locally on your device. We do not upload your videos or analysis results to a server unless future features explicitly ask for consent.

Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or share your personal data. CricLens uses on-device processing (MediaPipe) for pose estimation and stores results locally.

Your Choices

  • You can skip providing your name during onboarding.
  • You can delete recorded clips or exported videos from your device at any time.
  • You can revoke camera/microphone permissions in your device settings.

Children's Privacy

CricLens is intended for general audiences. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us to request deletion.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We will update the date at the top of this page to reflect the latest version.

Contact

For questions or requests, contact the CricLens team at privacy@criclens.app.
