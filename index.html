CricLens Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

CricLens helps you analyze cricket performance using video and motion-driven training tools. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Feature Updates Covered in This Version

  • Expanded analysis modes for batting and bowling workflows.
  • Player evaluation history, timelines, and rankings stored on-device.
  • Mini-games and training experiences including BatSense and Shot Trainer.
  • Optional notifications (local and cloud messaging) for app events and updates.
  • Optional premium and ad-supported experiences.

Information We Collect

  • Account & onboarding data: Your name (optional) and basic profile preferences to personalize your experience.
  • Videos you import or record: Used to run analysis and generate results.
  • Analysis outputs: Pose frames, metrics, feedback, scores, tips, and session history stored on-device.
  • Training & mini-game activity: Motion samples, swing events, mini-game stats, and progress records stored on-device.
  • Notifications data: If enabled, device notification tokens and related metadata may be processed to deliver push notifications.
  • Device permissions: Camera, microphone, notifications, and storage/media access depending on features you use.

How We Use Your Data

  • Run on-device cricket analysis and training feedback engines.
  • Save sessions, player evaluations, and progress so you can review your history.
  • Power mini-games, rankings, and performance timeline features.
  • Send optional local or push notifications when you opt in.
  • Export analysis overlays and related outputs to your device when requested.
  • Support app quality, reliability, and feature improvements.

Data Storage

CricLens primarily stores analysis sessions, metrics, preferences, and training progress locally on your device. We do not upload your videos or core analysis results to our own servers by default. If a future cloud feature requires upload or sync, we will clearly request your consent in-app first.

Sharing, Ads, and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. CricLens uses third-party SDKs and platform services where needed, such as on-device ML processing, notifications infrastructure, and ad delivery for ad-supported experiences. These providers process data according to their own privacy terms.

Your Choices

  • You can skip optional profile fields such as your name.
  • You can delete recorded clips, exported files, and app data from your device at any time.
  • You can disable notifications and revoke camera/microphone/storage permissions in your device settings.
  • You can choose whether to use premium-only or ad-supported features where available.

Data Retention

Data stored locally remains on your device until you delete it, clear app data, or uninstall the app. Notification-related identifiers are retained only as long as needed for notification delivery or as required by platform services.

Children's Privacy

CricLens is intended for general audiences. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us to request deletion.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as CricLens evolves. We will update the date at the top of this page to reflect the latest version.
